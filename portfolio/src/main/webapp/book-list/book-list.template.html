Notice when you click the links on this page what happens to the URLs at the top of the page.

Hopefully you see that the only stuff changing comes after what's known as the hash bang (#!).

Parameters after the hash bang are used by AngularJS to route you to certain views (like the book list or book detail).

You can look at app.config.js to see where we tell AngularJS which URL corresponds to which view.
